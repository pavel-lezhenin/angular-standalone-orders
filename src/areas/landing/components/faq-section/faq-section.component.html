Frequently Asked Questions

How does offline mode work?

Our platform uses IndexedDB to store your data locally in the browser, allowing you to continue working even without an internet connection. All changes are automatically synchronized when you're back online.

What user roles are supported?

We support three main roles: Users (view and create orders), Managers (approve and modify orders), and Administrators (full system access including user management and configuration).

Can I integrate with my existing systems?

Yes! Our platform provides a comprehensive REST API that allows you to integrate with your existing CRM, ERP, or other business systems. We also support webhooks for real-time event notifications.

Is my data secure?

Absolutely. We use industry-standard encryption for data at rest and in transit. Role-based access control ensures that users only see data they're authorized to access. Regular security audits keep your data protected.
